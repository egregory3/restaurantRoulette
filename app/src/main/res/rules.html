The Rules

This app is rooted in the words "I don't know" or "Whatever you want".
As long as there have been restaurants the words "Where do you want to eat
have been followed by "Wherever" leading to a never ending cycle of confusion.

If you are brave enough to spin the wheel of Restaurant Roulette then all that confusion can come to an end.
The rules are simple. You and your partner or friends agree to let restaurant roulette choose where you'll eat tonight
Will it be spicy Thai food, a hot dog shack, or a fancy five start restaurant? Only the wheel knows.

If you're brave enough to trust your culinary experience to retaurant roulette, just click the roulette wheel below.
